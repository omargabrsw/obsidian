## **Workout Notes (Before Training)**

### **Training Fundamentals:**

- **Skills First** - Always start with handstand holds
- **3 sets per exercise** with perfect form
- **Stop when reaching** technical failure for skills
- **3+ min rest between sets** for full recovery
- **Progressive overload** - Try to improve every session
- **Quality > Quantity** - Perfect form builds the Adonis physique

### **Recovery & Fatigue Management**

✅ **Listen to your body** – if form breaks down, stop early.

✅ **Good sleep = better recovery & strength.**

✅ **Stay hydrated & eat enough protein for muscle recovery.**

### **General Training Plan**

- **Skill Work First → Strength Training → Core Work**
- **Warm-up with dynamic stretching & mobility.**
- **Cooldown with flexibility work or light movement.**
- **Cycle through this routine for 3 cycles.**
