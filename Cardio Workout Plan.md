## Round 1 — Footwork & Rhythm

**Goal:** Warm up movement and stance discipline.

Focus on:

- Bounce and stance switching
- Forward/backward movement
- Step-in jab
- Pivot after every combo

Example flow:

- Jab → step out
- Jab → cross → pivot
- Feint → jab → move

Rules:

- No power
- Stay relaxed
- Never stand still more than 2 seconds

---

## Round 2 — Jab Control

**Goal:** Build a dangerous lead hand.

Focus:

- Jab to head
- Jab to body
- Double jab
- Jab → teep

Example combos:

- Jab → jab → cross
- Jab → lead hook → cross
- Jab → rear teep

Visualization:

Control distance like you're fighting a taller opponent.

---

## Round 3 — Boxing Combinations

**Goal:** Hand speed and boxing transitions.

Example combos:

- Jab → cross → hook
- Jab → cross → hook → cross
- Cross → hook → cross

Rules:

- Always **exit after combos**
- Add slips or rolls

Example:

Slip → cross → hook → pivot out

---

## Round 4 — Kicks Integration

**Goal:** Blend punches with kicks.

Example combos:

- Jab → cross → rear kick
- Cross → hook → lead kick
- Jab → rear kick

Important:

**Return to stance quickly after every kick.**

Add:

- Kick → step out
- Kick → check

---

## Round 5 — Teep & Distance Control

**Goal:** Muay Thai distance management.

Focus:

- Lead teep
- Rear teep
- Fake teep → punch

Examples:

- Teep → jab → cross
- Teep → rear kick
- Fake teep → cross

Imagine opponent rushing you.

---

## Round 6 — Defensive Round

**Goal:** Reaction and defense.

Add after every combo:

- Slip
- Roll
- Check kick
- Step back

Example flow:

Opponent throws → you defend → counter.

Example:

Slip → cross → hook → kick

---

## Round 7 — Counter Fighting

**Goal:** Think like a fighter.

Visualize opponent attacking.

Examples:

Opponent jabs → slip → cross

Opponent kicks → check → cross

Opponent rushes → teep

Key rule:

**You must defend first before every attack.**

---

## Round 8 — Clinch Entries

**Goal:** Muay Thai specialty.

Shadow:

- Enter clinch
- Knee strikes
- Turn opponent
- Exit

Example flow:

Jab → cross → step in → clinch → knee ×3 → push away → kick

---

## Round 9 — Fight Simulation

**Goal:** Mix everything.

Use:

- Punches
- Kicks
- Knees
- Teeps
- Clinch
- Defense

Rule:

No repeating the same combo twice.

Think **real fight pacing**.

---

## Round 10 — Power Round

**Goal:** Finish strong.

Every combo:

**Full power, full speed.**

Examples:

- Jab → cross → hook → kick
- Cross → hook → cross → knee
- Jab → cross → lead hook → rear kick

Add explosive movement.

---

# Technical Rules (Important)

1. **Always return guard to face**
2. **Exhale on strikes**
3. **Pivot after combos**
4. **Never square your hips**
5. **Imagine a real opponent**

Shadowboxing is **mental sparring**, not cardio.
